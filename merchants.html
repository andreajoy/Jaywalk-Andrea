Reward Customers

Jaywalk is a new app that lets you reward your customers for physical activity. They come in and send you their earned JayCoin and you give them a discount. We’re waking up Main Street and we need your help! Join Jaywalk today.

Get discovered

Online Advertising, Social Media, Paid Reviews, Print Ads, Chalkboards—they cost a lot. Are they really bringing in more customers or getting them to come back?

You need something different. Something that simplifies rewards and motivates people to get out of the house and into your place of business.

Meet Jaywalk for Merchants:

  1. Have your customized ad showcased in the home feed of nearby users
  2. Eye catching logo marker on the Merchant Map and List
  3. Track all of the data of your transactions through a personal dashboard

Simple Transactions

Users earn JayCoin credits for walking, sharing photos and descriptions about local places, voting on posts and inviting other users to join Jaywalk. Their credits expire at midnight, so they will have a reason to get out and spend every day.

Customers send you $1 in JayCoin through the app and you give them a discount of $1. You can accept more or even add multipliers depending on your business needs (i.e. $1 Jaycoin = $2 off). You set the rules and can change them as needed.


Manage your bill

The great news is for every $1 in JayCoin you redeem from your customers, we’ll match it and reduce your bill with us.

So if you start out at the $29.99 level and redeem $20 in Jaycoin credits in the first month you’ll only have to pay $9.99. If you have a lot more business you can start at a higher tier. At any time, you may upgrade your plan to increase JayCoin redemptions.

The monthly fee is not charged at signup. You have 30 days to redeem JayCoin to get your bill down to the minimum monthly payment for your plan.
